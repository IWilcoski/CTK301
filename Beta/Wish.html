Wish Upon A Star

Engine Used: RenPy

Language Used: Python

Wish Upon A Star is a project from ISU's Fall 2024 Game Jam in which I worked as lead and almost sole programmer. It involved getting to know and gaining approval from four different characters from different universes, and a final choice between them. The project was my first time with RenPy, and my first time using Python at ISU. The project involved tracking approval ratings and progress through various story lines.
