Creating A Landscape

Engine Used: Unreal

Language Used: Blueprint

My Creating A Landscape project was one of my first style oriented Unreal projects. It focused on both developing a landscape and creating a way to traverse it, more specifically using mountain roads and a path through the woods in my case. In addition, elements like footsteps unique to the ground type were implemented to help in immersion, and a tunnel through the landscape tested my ability to insert objects through organic shapes.
