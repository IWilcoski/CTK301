Bugged and Sorted

Engine Used: Unity

Language Used: C++

Bugged and sorted is a project from ISU's Winter Break 2024 Game Jam in which I served as the sole programmer. It involved dragging robots off of conveyor belts and sorting them into various bins based on characteristics like wheels, radar, flight, screens, and defects. The project involved several sprite animations, a handful of simple sound effects for successes and failures, and score management.
